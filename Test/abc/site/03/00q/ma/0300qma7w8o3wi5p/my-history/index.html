My History

My paintings that started were started in Italy and finished in the US have become the subject of many different solo and group exhibitions including “Intimacy” at the Kunst Museum.


People seem to like what I paint, so my works oftentimes  end up hanging in either public or private collection, including the Savannah College of Arts, the Somerset Art Association.

“But my only sincere intention is to simply transfer the intimacy and vulnerability of my human experience into a painted surface.”


Being a native of new york, it took me quite a lot of time, east and greenwich village experience and day-and-night painting practice to understand, that turning the painting from a mere hobby to a full-time employment day job is what i need to do!

My name is Dominica Boivin and I’m a professional fine artist based in Los Angeles, CA. I take inspiration from the innovative styles of contemporary and urban art, such as graphic and graffiti illustrations. I am always open to new artistic challenges and collaborations. Feel free to contact me if you need a unique, one-of-a-kind artwork.
